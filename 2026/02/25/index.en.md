## Parties agree on future savings

First, news about government savings.

The next government will have to save money by 8...11 billion euros. All parties except the Left Alliance agree on the savings.

The Finnish state economy is now in deficit by 4.5 percent. According to EU rules, the state economy may not be in deficit by more than 3 percent.

The Finnish state is therefore producing much less money than it spends. The parties have agreed that the next government will save up to 11 billion euros.

Finally, about lighthouses.
