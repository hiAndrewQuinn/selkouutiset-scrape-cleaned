## Water

Now some news about water.

Water levels have dropped in many places in Finland.

The reason is that rainfall has decreased.

In North Savo, the water utility in Joroinen has asked people to save water.

Low groundwater levels can also make crop production difficult if there is not enough rain in the summer. Problems can also arise elsewhere in Finland.

Water levels have dropped in many places in Finland.

There are also low water levels in Lapland.

For example, the level of Lake Kemijärvi has been low for many weeks.

Due to low water levels, the Kemijoki hydroelectric power plant has reduced its flow to ensure that water enters the lake.
