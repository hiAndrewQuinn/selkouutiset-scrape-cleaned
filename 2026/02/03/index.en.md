## Waste import

Now the news about the import of waste into Finland.

There is criminal activity in waste imports.

A lot of waste is imported to Finland from abroad.

A large part of the waste comes from Italy.

Yle investigated waste companies. It turned out that waste was imported into Finland by an Italian company whose directors have been convicted of illegal waste transportation and bribery.

The company's name is Sicula Trasporti.

The Finnish Environment Institute was not aware of the company's criminal record. That's why the company was given permission to import waste into Finland.

Chief Inspector **Hannele Nikander** says that the Environmental Center trusted the Italian authorities.

*"We generally and fundamentally trust the inspections carried out by the authorities of that country, in this case Italy."*

There is criminal activity in the import of waste into Finland.

Experts say that the returns in the waste sector are high and the risk of getting caught is low.
