Helsinki Garden project

First, about the new arena project.

The Helsinki Garden project is generating a lot of discussion.

The government has decided that the project will receive 35 million euros in conditional support from the state.

Prime Minister **Petteri Orpo** says that the initiative to support the arena came from the Coalition Party. Orpo denies the claim that the support decision is a favor to the Coalition Party's election funders.

The opposition in particular has demanded justification for the decision.

The Ministry of Finance says that there is no estimate of the jobs and economic impact of the Helsinki Garden project.

The National Audit Office says it is closely monitoring the public debate.

## Women in the military

Then news from the army.

Almost 40 percent of women experience inappropriate treatment in military service. It is most commonly bullying or sexual harassment.

Less than 10 percent of men experience inappropriate behavior.

Every year, 20,000 young people complete military service. Of these, only about 800 are women. The service is voluntary for women.

Almost 40 percent of women experience inappropriate treatment in military service.
