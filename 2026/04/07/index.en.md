## Work and artificial intelligence

Initially, the topic is work and artificial intelligence.

Only a small proportion of Finns are afraid of artificial intelligence in working life.

This is according to the Finnish Economic Research Institute Etla.

Etla asked for opinions from those who are already using artificial intelligence at work.

Only 8 percent of respondents feared that artificial intelligence could replace their own work.

The number of people who are afraid has not increased in 5 years, even though the use of artificial intelligence has increased significantly at the same time.

Finns are not afraid of artificial intelligence in working life.

Instead, many AI users say that the work is important and inspiring.

## Moonlight

Then the topic is space flight.

Astronauts have orbited the moon.

The Artemis 2 lunar mission took off from the USA last week. There are 4 astronauts on board.

The astronauts lost contact with Earth for 40 minutes when the ship was behind the moon.

At the same time, a new record was set. A human has never been this far from Earth before. The distance between the ship and Earth was over 400,000 kilometers.

Astronauts have orbited the moon.

The ship is expected back to Earth on Friday.

## Pandas

Finally, the topic is pandas, which are familiar to Finns.

Panda bears Lumi and Pyry have found a new home in China.

Finns got to know the pandas at Ähtäri Zoo. However, the pandas were returned to China over a year ago.

Now Lumi and Pyry live in a new panda center in Sichuan. They have been given new Chinese names.

The pandas' enclosure is similar to their habitat in the wild.

Panda bears Lumi and Pyry have found a new home in China.

The new research center houses a total of 20 giant pandas. Researchers hope that more pandas will be born in the coming years.
