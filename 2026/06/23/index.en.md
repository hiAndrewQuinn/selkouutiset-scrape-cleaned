## Only a few wild salmon in the Bay of Bothnia

Finally, some news about salmon fishing.

There are only a few wild salmon in the Bay of Bothnia.

Professional fishermen are only allowed to catch salmon in small quantities.

If a fisherman catches wild salmon, it must be released into the sea. Only planted salmon may be fished.

Many fishermen are concerned that wild salmon are declining in the Bay of Bothnia.

Scientists don't know the exact reason for the decline. However, scientists say that wild salmon need free-flowing rivers where they can reproduce.

There are fewer good salmon rivers in Finland than before.
