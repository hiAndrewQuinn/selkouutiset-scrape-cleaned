## Russia sanctions and Wärtsilä

First, news about circumventing Russian sanctions.

Russia continues to receive Wärtsilä spare parts for ships.

Spare parts are used in oil tankers, icebreakers and other large vessels.

Because of sanctions, technology cannot be exported to Russia. That's why Russia is circumventing the sanctions.

First, the spare parts are exported to another country, for example the United Arab Emirates or China.

They are then delivered to Russia through many companies.

Russia continues to receive Wärtsilä spare parts for ships.

Wärtsilä's General Counsel **Nora Steiner-Forsberg** says that Wärtsilä itself no longer sells products to Russia.

Wärtsilä does not want parts to be exported to Russia, but it is difficult to prevent the activity.

## European Capital of Culture Oulu

Finally, cultural news.

Oulu is the European Capital of Culture this year.

The opening ceremony saw many great performances.

Approximately 250,000 people attended the opening weekend events.

President **Alexander Stubb** gave a speech.

Oulu is the European Capital of Culture together with 39 municipalities.

There are many events in Oulu and the municipalities throughout the year.

This year, the second European Capital of Culture is Trenčín, located in Slovakia.
