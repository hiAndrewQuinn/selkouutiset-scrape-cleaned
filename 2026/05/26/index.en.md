## Renting Wolt accounts

Now the topic is the food delivery company Wolt.

Food delivery company Wolt is preventing food delivery accounts from being rented. Wolt says that accounts will no longer be rented in August.

It's hard to get a job as a Wolt food delivery person, so many people rented a work account from another delivery person.

Many Wolt couriers have paid hundreds of euros per month in account rent

The accounts have been rented for years, but rents have increased recently.
