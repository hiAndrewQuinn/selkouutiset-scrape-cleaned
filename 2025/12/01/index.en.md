## Taxis

Finally, news about taxis.

All taxis will be required to have a taximeter.

The government will present a proposal for a new taxi law.

The matter has been discussed in the government for a long time.

The Finns Party and the National Coalition Party have disagreed on the matter.

The Finns Party has wanted taxi meters in taxis because they reduce the grey economy.

The Coalition Party has feared that mandatory meters could reduce the number of taxis and competition.

All taxis will be required to have a taximeter. Taxis will also have colored taxi license plates.

Transport Minister **Lulu Ranne** says that the taximeter will restore confidence in the taxi industry and improve supervision.

*"This will eradicate the grey economy, taxi meters will play a big role in that and supervision will also be significantly strengthened."*
