## People in Finland die earlier from cancer

Finally, news about cancer treatments.

In Finland, people die of many cancers earlier than in other Nordic countries.

For example, people with lung cancer in Norway and Sweden live longer than in Finland.

This was revealed in research by Yle's MOT program.

Doctors and patients told MOT's reporter that it can be difficult to get treatment quickly in Finland. Then the cancer has time to spread and is difficult to treat.

In Finland, people die of cancer earlier than in other Nordic countries.

The researcher says that it is also difficult to obtain new and expensive cancer drugs in Finland.

In other Nordic countries, expensive cancer drugs are more easily available.
