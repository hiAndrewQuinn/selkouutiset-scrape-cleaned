## Language teaching for immigrants

First, news about language teaching for immigrants.

There may be major cuts to language teaching for immigrants.

The government plans to cut 46 million euros from integration funds.

The cut will reduce Finnish language teaching in particular.

In the future, language training for immigrants will be part of municipal workforce training.

For example, community colleges no longer receive government money for language training for immigrants.

Last year, almost 60,000 immigrants studied at colleges.

There may be major cuts to language teaching for immigrants.

Experts say that the cuts will make it harder for immigrants to find employment, which could lead to an increase in unemployment.

Many college teachers may also lose their jobs.
