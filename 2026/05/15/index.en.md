## Clothing manufacturing in Finland

Finally, some news about clothing manufacturing in Finland.

Entrepreneur **Marleena Lehtonen** makes clothes in Helsinki.

All clothes are sewn in Finland, although sewing is more expensive than abroad.

For example, sewing a dress costs 39 euros in Finland and 15 euros in Estonia.

Lehtonen buys surplus silk from luxury clothing brands in Italy.

Lehtonen says that domestic work is important, even if the income is low.

Marleena Lehtonen wants to show that clothes can still be made in Finland.
