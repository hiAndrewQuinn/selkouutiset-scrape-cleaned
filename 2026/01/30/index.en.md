## Nursing home death

First, news about a nursing home for the elderly.

A serious error has occurred at a nursing home in Espoo.

The resident died because the alarm did not go off.

The resident tried to call for help with the safety bracelet 11 times in an hour.

The alarm did not reach the scene because the nurses' phones were not on.

There are two emergency phones. Both phones had run out of battery.

No one had charged their phones.

A resident died at the Attendo nursing home in Espoo over the weekend because the alarms did not go off.

Attendo management says the staff has made a serious mistake.

The police begin investigating the case.

The Espoo case is the second nursing home death in a short period of time.

An elderly person strangled to death in a Turku nursing home in December.
