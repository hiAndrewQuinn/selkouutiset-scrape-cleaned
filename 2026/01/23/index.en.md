## Free benefits at small high schools

Then news about small high schools.

There is a shortage of students in upper secondary schools in small municipalities. Upper secondary schools attract students with free benefits.

## They want to ban children's smartphones

Next news about children's phone use.

Finnish health authorities want to ban smartphones from children.

The National Institute for Health and Welfare recommends that children under the age of 13 not use smartphones or social media.

Silja Kosola, an associate professor of adolescent medicine, says that in Finland, smartphones are given to children who are too young.

Kosola supports Prime Minister Petteri Orpo's proposal to completely ban social media for those under 15 years of age.
