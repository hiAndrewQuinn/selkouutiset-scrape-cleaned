## Finnish companies and Trump

Then about the economy.

US President Donald Trump's policies are worrying Finnish companies.

Finnish Entrepreneurs conducted a survey. Every second small and medium-sized company said that US economic policy affects their operations.

It is influenced by trade relations between Europe and the USA.

Trump's climate policy and Russia's war of aggression in Ukraine are also worrying Finnish companies.

Entrepreneurs who support the National Coalition Party are most worried.

## Mortgages

News about mortgages.

It is more difficult than before to get a mortgage for old homes. This is what many real estate agents across Finland say.

It is more difficult to get a loan if major repairs are planned for a single-family home or housing association.

However, banks Nordea and OP Pohjola say that no changes have been made to access to loans.

## Disability benefits

Finally, about support for the disabled.

Subsidized holidays for families with disabilities will end after this year.

The reason is government savings. The Social and Health Organization Assistance Center will stop funding holiday organizations.

The last subsidized holiday was organized in Vuokatti, Kainuu. There were 7 families on holiday.
