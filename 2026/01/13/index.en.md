## GNSS interference

Initially, the topic is satellite positioning.

Satellite positioning jamming, or GNSS jamming, has increased in Finland in recent years.

The disruption is detrimental to, for example, air and maritime traffic.

Satellite positioning allows planes and ships to know exactly where they are and where they are going.

A study by the National Land Survey of Finland shows that GNSS interference has increased, and it is especially common in Eastern Finland.

The harassment originates from Russia.

## Coffee hoarding

Finally, let's talk about coffee.

Finns are hoarding more coffee than before.

Hoarding means buying many packages of coffee to stock up when there is a good offer in the store.

For example, S Group stores sold about 20 percent more coffee on sale in December than a year earlier. K Group stores have also noticed that coffee offers are of interest to customers.

People are hoarding discount coffee because the price of coffee has risen a lot, and their own financial situation may have tightened.
