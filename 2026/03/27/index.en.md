## Egg shortage

First, some news about eggs.

There is now a shortage of eggs in Finland.

The producer organization MTK says that the reason for the egg shortage is the increase in exports.

Exports have increased because Finnish eggs are paid a better price abroad than at home, says MTK.

Stores believe that the egg shortage is due to Finns buying more eggs than before.

There has been a shortage of eggs in stores recently.

Temporary shortages may also be caused by the fact that many farms are changing chickens.

When the egg production of old hens declines, they are replaced with new birds.
