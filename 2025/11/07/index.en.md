## Election cloud service

Now the news about the elections.

Finnish election information systems are being transferred abroad.

The voter register and candidate registers will be transferred to a foreign cloud service.

Election results information will also be transferred there.

The system is located in Sweden and is owned by the American company Amazon Web Services.

The reform will be introduced in the next parliamentary elections in 2027.

Finnish election information systems are being transferred abroad.

The reason is money. Election Director **Arto Jääskeläinen** says that the cloud service saves money.

Information security researcher **Harri Hursti** is concerned about the change.

He says that it is not a good idea to store data abroad.

Hursti says that elections are not the right place to save money.

## Child protection

Finally, news about child protection.

The need for foster care is growing in Finland.

This is according to the Finnish Institute for Health and Welfare.

Researchers have noticed that there are more children in foster care than before.

For example, in Central Finland there are 25 children in high-level child welfare institutions. Last year there were 10 children.

The need for foster care is growing in Finland.

Reasons for foster care include, for example, the child's mental health problems, substance abuse problems, or crimes.

It is possible that general rush or, for example, the war in Ukraine are increasing children's nausea.
