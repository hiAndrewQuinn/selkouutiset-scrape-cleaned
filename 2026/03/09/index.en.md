## Finnish evacuation flight

Then the topic is the evacuation flight.

Finns have arrived at night from the war zone in the Middle East.

The Ministry for Foreign Affairs arranged a flight from Oman for the Finns. The flight landed at Helsinki-Vantaa Airport at night.

There were just over 160 passengers on the plane. 70 of them were children.

## Iran's new leader

Finally, news from Iran.

Iran has a new supreme leader.

The new Supreme Leader is 56-year-old **Mojtaba Khamenei**.

Mojtaba Khamenei is the son of the previous Supreme Leader **Ali Khamenei**. The elder Khamenei was killed in US and Israeli strikes.

The Supreme Leader decides Iran's policies and the use of military force. Experts say Mojtaba Khamenei is a hard-line leader.

Iran has a new Supreme Leader. The choice was made by Iran's religious leaders.
