More countries are participating in the World Championships than ever before. There are 48 countries participating, compared to 32 teams 4 years ago. Norway and Sweden are participating from the Nordic countries. Finland did not qualify for the championships through the qualifiers.

The World Cup starts with the preliminary rounds. There are 12 preliminary rounds. A total of 32 countries advance from the preliminary rounds. After this, the knockout rounds are played, in which the winner of each match advances.

The final will be played on July 19th in the USA. The venue is Metlife Stadium in New Jersey.

The World Cup was first played in 1930. The tournament is played every 4 years.

4 years ago, Argentina won the World Cup. Brazil has won the most championships, with 5. Germany and Italy have won the tournament 4 times.

This time, the early favorites include Spain, France and England.
