## Finnish cattle are sold abroad

First, news about beef.

A lot of cattle is sold abroad from Finland, even though there is a shortage of beef in Finland.

Cattle are sold abroad because they are paid more abroad than in Finland.

Last year, almost 400 live cattle were sold abroad from Finland. That's more than ever before.

Cattle living abroad are well paid, partly because halal-slaughtered meat is popular.

Beef is paid less in Finland than abroad, which is why a lot of cattle is sold abroad.
