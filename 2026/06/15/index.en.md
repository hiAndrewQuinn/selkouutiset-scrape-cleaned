## Employee pension contributions may decrease

Then the news about pension payments.

Last year, 24.9% of an employee's salary went to pension contributions. Pension contributions are paid to pension funds.

Now the State Pension Agency says that contributions can be reduced.

The reason is a new rule. The new rule allows pension funds to invest money more easily. Pension contributions do not have to be increased if the investments yield good returns.

The State Pension Agency says that pension contributions can be reduced.

Previously, employees' pension contributions had to be increased to provide enough money for people's pensions.
