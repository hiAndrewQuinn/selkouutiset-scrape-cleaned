## Finnish education

The first topic is Finnish language teaching.

Trade unions want more Finnish language instruction for immigrants who come to Finland for work. It is difficult for immigrants to get jobs if their language skills are poor.

**Vanessa Phekani** moved to Finland from Malawi, Africa. She has lived in Oulu for 7 years. Phekani has not been able to find work in Finland, even though she has a good education and a lot of work experience:

-I'm looking for different jobs. It's difficult because I don't speak Finnish well.

## Schools

Now news about school buildings.

More schools are being demolished in Finland than before. Over 100 schools are demolished in Finland every year. Many school buildings have mold. There are not enough students in the schools. There are currently about 2,000 comprehensive schools in Finland.

More schools are being demolished in Finland than before. Experts say that many school buildings will also be demolished in the coming years.
