## School

Next, news from school.

Finnish comprehensive schools need to be changed. This is what a report from the Ministry of Education says. Comprehensive schools need to be changed because there will be fewer students in schools in the future than there are now.

The Ministry of Education's report says that the number of schools should be reduced. Municipalities should increase cooperation, for example by having shared teachers. Schools can have more distance learning than they do now.

The comprehensive school system needs to change. The Ministry of Education's report says that schools should be given the same amount of money in the future as they are now.
