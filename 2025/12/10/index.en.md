## Kirkkonummi rejected the new railway line

Then the news about the new train line, the Western Line.

The new train line from Helsinki to Turku is in trouble.

The municipality of Kirkkonummi decided that it would not pay for the construction of the new railway line. Kirkkonummi's share of the new railway line was 15 million euros.

The Minister of Transport says that other municipalities must now pay Kirkkonummi's share. Otherwise, the new railway line will not be built.

Other municipalities on the Western Line include Helsinki, Espoo Vihti, Lohja, Salo and Turku.

Espoo, Vihti and Turku have already decided that they will pay their share of the western line.

## Australia bans social media from young people

Finally, a news about social media.

Australia bans the use of social media by those under 16.

Australia is the first country to ban the use of social media by children and young people.

Australia now has a law on the matter. The law requires social media companies to block children and young people from accessing Tiktok, YouTube, Instagram or Facebook.

The Australian government justifies the ban by saying that social media is harmful to children and young people.

Many young people oppose the ban because social media is a big part of young people's lives.

Australia bans the use of social media by those under 16.
