## Patient records

Now, news about healthcare.

There are variations and errors in patient data recording.

**Ronja Pöyhönen** from Turku noticed this when she read her own information on the Omakanta service.

The information said that Pöyhönen had a mental health problem even though he had a brain tumor.

Aalto University has examined patient data.

Researcher **Saija Simola** says that some of the entries are inappropriate and may offend the patient.

For example, a doctor may write that the patient is difficult or mentally ill.

Due to incorrect information, the patient may receive the wrong treatment.

Researchers have now given doctors instructions on how to record the information.
