## Wellbeing areas

First, news about politics.

The opposition parties want a joint working group for all parties. The purpose of the working group is to solve the economic problems of welfare areas.

Opposition parties are parliamentary parties that are not in government.

Many welfare areas are facing a difficult economic situation, which could worsen health services for Finns.

That is why the opposition parties want an all-party working group to solve the economic problems of the welfare areas.

They request that Prime Minister **Petteri Orpo** establish such a working group.

The chairwoman of the Left Alliance, **Minja Koskela**, says that welfare areas should be given more time to make savings.

Currently, the deadline for savings is already next year.
