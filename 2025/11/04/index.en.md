## Stubb: The importance of nuclear weapons has increased

First, the news about nuclear weapons.

Finnish President **Alexander Stubb** says that the importance of nuclear weapons is growing.

For example, Russia and North Korea are testing nuclear weapons. The US has also announced that it intends to test nuclear weapons.

President Stubb wants to prevent the use of nuclear weapons. He proposes a peace meeting.

He suggests that the US and Russian presidents meet in South Africa in November, where the G20 summit of rich countries will be held.

Stubb spoke about nuclear weapons and the peace conference at a national defense course.

## Independence Day celebrates education

Education and culture are being celebrated at the Presidential Palace during this year's Independence Day celebration.

The presidential couple says that they are inviting teachers in particular to the celebration.

You can watch the Independence Day celebration on December 6th on TV1. This year, the program starts at 6:15 PM.

## New website for Finnish students: yle.fi/opisuomea

Then news about learning Finnish.

Yle has launched a new website where you can learn Finnish.

The website is called Opi suomea (link to the website: yle.fi/opisuomea). There you can learn Finnish in many ways. For example, you can learn spoken Finnish from popular TV programs.

**Jussi Visti** is one teacher who has done assignments for the Learn Finnish website:

– First, maybe you should just listen. A bit like you would listen to music, listen to spoken language to get into the rhythm. The tasks will help you learn to find words and the most important things.

The Learn Finnish website has many different TV programs and tasks. You can also play a word pyramid game on the site. In the game, you learn Finnish words.

**Riya Rani Saha** has learned Finnish on the Learn Finnish website:
