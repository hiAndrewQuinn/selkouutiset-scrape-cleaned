## Forest industry jobs are decreasing

Next, news about jobs in the forest industry.

Forest industry jobs are decreasing in Finland.

Large forestry company MetsäGroup plans to lay off over 400 employees in Finland.

MetsäGroup's mills process wood into products such as sawn timber and pulp. Pulp is used to make paper and cardboard.

MetsäGroup says it is reducing jobs because pulp in particular is currently selling poorly.

Äänekoski factory manager Timo Tallinen says the situation is sad.

## MP in court over gun

The prosecutor believes that Vornanen committed a firearms offense, assaulted and threatened, and caused a dangerous situation.

Vornanen says he shot the ground because he was threatened. He says he has a permit to carry a gun.

Vornanen has worked as a police officer.
