## Apartments

Now news about apartments.

Young adults are considering buying a home less often than before. The survey shows that 30 percent of people under 40 are considering buying their first home. In 2020, this number was 40 percent.

Young adults think that their salary is not good enough. Young people are also concerned about the development of the Finnish economy. Young people also think that owning their own home is not a good investment.

Fewer young adults are considering buying their own home than before. However, young people often dream of having their own home someday.

## Ambulance cameras

Now the topic is an ambulance.

Ambulances are equipped with cameras more often than before. The camera records images from around the ambulance.

Sometimes the camera image shows something that interests the police.

**Jari Illukka** from the Helsinki Police:

-Whenever there is footage available, we get an idea of what happened. We can also use the footage to search for the perpetrator.

There is a camera in the ambulance more often than before. The camera records the surroundings of the ambulance, but sometimes also the treatment room. The camera is inside the ambulance for the safety of the nurses.
