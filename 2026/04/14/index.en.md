## The price of water may increase

First, some news about water.

Water is too cheap in Finland, say high-ranking authorities.

An official from the Ministry of Agriculture and Forestry says people need to pay more for water. Water utilities need more money to repair water pipes.

Some of the water pipes are in really bad condition. Broken water pipes can leak dirty sewage into the environment. Drinking water can also be contaminated if the pipes are broken.

The new law requires that water utilities must have money to repair water pipes.

In Finland, the authorities believe that water is too cheap.

The new law could raise water prices. People will have to pay more for water so that water utilities have money to repair water pipes.
